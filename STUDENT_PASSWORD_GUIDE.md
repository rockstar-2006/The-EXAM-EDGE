# 🎓 How Students Login - Quick Reference

## **Your Password = Your USN**

The system has been simplified. You no longer need an OTP to set up your account.

### **Step-by-Step Login Process:**

1. **Open Faculty Quest App**
2. **Click "Login"**
3. **Enter Your Email Address**
   - Example: `student123@college.com`
4. **Enter Your Password**
   - **Default Password**: Your **USN** (provided by your teacher)
   - Example USN: `1OX21CS001`
5. **Click "Login"**
6. ✅ **Done!** You are now logged in and can see your quizzes.

---

## **⚠️ PASSWORD TIPS**

- Your **USN** is your default password.
- It is case-sensitive, so type it exactly as given by your faculty.
- You can use your USN to login **every time** unless you choose to change your password in the settings.

---

## **If You Forgot Your Password**

If you changed your password and forgot it:

1. **On Login Page**
2. **Click "Forgot Password"**
3. **Enter Email**: `student123@college.com`
4. **Click "Send Reset Link"**
5. ✉️ **Check email** for reset link
6. **Click link** in email
7. **Create new password** (Must have uppercase, lowercase, number, and 8+ chars)
8. **Login with new password**

---

## **Common Problems & Solutions**

### **❌ "Invalid Credentials"**
- ❌ Wrong USN or Email - check with your teacher
- ✅ **Solution**: Ensure your Email is correct and your USN is typed correctly as the password.

### **❌ "Email Not Found"**
- ❌ Your email is not registered in the system by any teacher.
- ✅ **Solution**: Contact your faculty to ensure they have added you to their student list.

### **❌ "Account Locked"**
- ❌ Too many failed login attempts (5+)
- ✅ **Solution**: Wait 30 minutes and try again.

---

## **Key Points to Remember**

1. **Your Email = Your Username**
   - Your unique identifier in the system.

2. **Your USN = Your Default Password**
   - No need to wait for OTPs or setup emails.
   - Just use the USN given by your teacher.

3. **Once Login = Easy Access**
   - Your session lasts for 7 days.
   - You will be automatically logged in until the session expires.
