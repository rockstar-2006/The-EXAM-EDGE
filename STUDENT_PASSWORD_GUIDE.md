# 🎓 How Students Login - Quick Reference

## **For NEW Students** (First Time)

### **Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Open Faculty Quest App**
2. **Click "Login"**
3. **Enter Email Address**
   - Example: `student123@college.com`
4. **Enter ANY Password** (doesn't matter, it will fail)
5. **Click "Login"**
   
   You'll see message:
   ```
   "Your account needs to be set up first. 
    We will send you an OTP to create your password."
   ```

6. **Click "Go to Password Setup"**

7. **Request OTP**
   - Email: `student123@college.com`
   - Click **"Send OTP"**
   - ✉️ **Check email** (takes 5-10 seconds)

8. **You'll Receive Email with OTP**
   - Subject: `Your Password Setup OTP - Faculty Quest`
   - Contains: **6-digit code** (e.g., `3 4 5 8 2 1`)
   - **Valid for: 10 minutes**

9. **Enter OTP & Create Password**
   - Email: `student123@college.com`
   - OTP: `345821` (from email)
   - **New Password**: Create your own! ⬇️

---

## **⚠️ PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS**

Your password MUST have:
- ✅ **At least 8 characters** (no limit on max)
- ✅ **At least 1 UPPERCASE letter** (A-Z)
- ✅ **At least 1 lowercase letter** (a-z)
- ✅ **At least 1 number** (0-9)

### **valid Password Examples:**
- ✅ `MyQuiz@2024`
- ✅ `Student@Pass1`
- ✅ `Secure123Pass`
- ✅ `Quiz@College99`

### **❌ Invalid Password Examples:**
- ❌ `password` (no uppercase or number)
- ❌ `Password1` (too weak sounding)
- ❌ `12345678` (no letters)
- ❌ `Pass1` (only 5 characters)

1. **On Login Page**
2. **Click "Forgot Password"**
3. **Enter Email**: `student123@college.com`
4. **Click "Send Reset Link"**
5. ✉️ **Check email** for reset link
6. **Click link** in email
7. **Create new password** (same requirements as above)
8. **Login with new password**

## **Common Problems & Solutions**

### **❌ "Invalid OTP"**
- ❌ Wrong OTP code - check email again
- ❌ OTP already used - request new one
- ✅ **Solution**: Request new OTP, copy carefully

### **❌ "OTP Expired"**
- ❌ Took longer than 10 minutes
- ✅ **Solution**: Click "Request OTP" again

### **❌ "Too Many Attempts"**
- ❌ Tried OTP 3+ times incorrectly
- ✅ **Solution**: Request new OTP after 5 minutes

### **❌ "Password Too Weak"**
- ❌ Missing uppercase, lowercase, or number
- ✅ **Solution**: Use example: `Quiz@2024`

### **❌ "Email Not Found"**
- ❌ Email not registered in system
- ✅ **Solution**: Contact your faculty

### **❌ "Account Locked"**
- ❌ Too many failed login attempts (5+)
- ✅ **Solution**: Wait 30 minutes and try again

---

## **Key Points to Remember**

1. **Your Email = Your Username**
   - Never share your email
   - It's your unique identifier

2. **Your Password = Secret**
   - Must have: uppercase, lowercase, number
   - At least 8 characters
   - Don't share with anyone

3. **OTP = One-Time Code**
   - 6 digits sent to your email
   - Valid for 10 minutes only
   - Different every time you request

4. **Once Setup = Easy Login**
   - After first setup, just use email + password
   - Token lasts 7 days
   - Auto-logout after 7 days
